Macaroni Hamburg Casserole

Learn About Recipe

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni in boiling, salted water. Then drain.

  2. Fry onions in butter till lightly brown.

  3. Add ground beef and cook till browned.

  4. Grease a baking dish.

  5. Combine macaroni with cooked ingredients and add half the cheese. Mix well.

  6. Season with salt and pepper.

  7. Cover with remaining cheese.

  8. Bake at 375 F for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni 4 cups

  • Ground Beef 1/2 pound

  • Sliced Onions 1/2 cup

  • Canned Tomatoes 2 cups

  • Butter 2 tbsp

  • Cheddar Cheese 1/2 cup

Learn More

  This filling dish (like many other) was largely about making meat last longer. The dish was distributed along with many others in a pamphlet titled "How to Eat Well Though Rationed" and was part of a large campagin to keep Canadians fed and healthy through the war.
  This particular recipe has been adapted from the original to work better with conventional ovens and use a specific complementery cheese. Click below to find the original.
