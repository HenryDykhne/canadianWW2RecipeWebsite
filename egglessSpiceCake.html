Eggless Spice Cake

Learn About Recipe

Instructions

  1. Slice raisins/currants in half.

  2. Combine all ingredients except flour, baking powder and baking soda into a saucepan.

  3. Place over moderate heat and simmer for 5 minutes.

  4. Cool ingredients.

  5. Sift flour.

  6. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.

  7. Grease cake/loaf pan.

  8. Place mixture into pan and bake at bake at 350 F for 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar 2 cups

  • Corn Syrup 1/2 cup

  • Hot Water 2 cups

  • Lard 2 tbsp

  • Raisins/Currants 1 lb

  • Salt 1 tsp

  • Cinnamon 1 tsp

  • Nutmeg 1/2 tsp

  • Baking Powder 1/2 tsp

  • Baking Soda 1 tsp

  • Flour 2 cups

Learn More

  Originally called Canadian war cake when it origniated during the first world war, it made a return during world war two. Both times, it was a popular dish to send to troops overseas and a welcome boost to morale.
  Like other recipies showcased here, this one was designed with rationing in mind. Its eggless, milkless, butterless and does not even require icing! A real boon for those cooking during wartime rationing.
